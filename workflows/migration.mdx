# Migrating from Stripe

If you are already processing payments directly through Stripe and want to adopt Billy, you can migrate your existing subscriptions seamlessly without any customer downtime or re-authentication of credit cards.

## Migration Strategy

Because Billy acts as an abstraction layer over your *existing* Stripe account, migrating is a matter of syncing identifiers, not moving credit card data.

1. **Sync Products & Prices:** Billy automatically pulls all active Products and Prices from your connected Stripe account upon project creation.
2. **Import Customers:** For every existing customer in your database, call the Billy Customer creation endpoint, explicitly passing their existing Stripe ID:
   ```typescript
   await billy.customers.create({
     email: dbUser.email,
     name: dbUser.name,
     externalUserId: dbUser.id,
     stripeCustomerId: 'cus_existing123' // Crucial step!
   });
   ```
   By passing the `stripeCustomerId`, Billy links the new internal record directly to the existing Stripe vault.
3. **Webhooks Catch-up:** Once the customer is linked, any future renewals or invoice creations for their existing active subscriptions will be automatically caught by Billy's webhooks and synced into the Billy database.

<Warning>
Do not attempt to recreate subscriptions for existing users. Simply mapping the Customer ID is enough; Billy's webhooks will populate the subscription data upon the next billing event.
</Warning>
