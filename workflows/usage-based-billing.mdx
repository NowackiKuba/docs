# Usage-based Billing

Billy supports two primary models for usage-based billing: **Prepaid Token Ledgers** and **Postpaid Metered Billing**.

## Prepaid Token Ledgers (Credits)

This is the most common model for AI companies (e.g., OpenAI). Customers purchase or are granted a bucket of tokens, which are deducted as they consume resources.

**Workflow:**
1. Customer subscribes to the "Pro Plan", which grants 100,000 tokens per month.
2. Billy automatically deposits 100,000 tokens to the ledger every time the subscription renews.
3. Your application deducts tokens via `POST /api/tokens/deduct` as the user generates AI responses.
4. When the balance hits 0, you restrict access or prompt them to buy a "Top-up" (a one-time Price that adds more tokens).

## Postpaid Metered Billing (Stripe)

In a postpaid model (like AWS), the customer consumes resources for free throughout the month, and is charged for the exact amount at the end of the billing cycle.

**Workflow:**
1. Create a Price with `billingType: usage_based` and `usageType: metered`.
2. As the customer uses resources, report the usage to Billy (coming soon to the API) or directly to Stripe's Metering API.
3. At the end of the month, Stripe calculates the total and generates an invoice automatically.
